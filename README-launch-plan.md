# Cypress Pup Co. — Launch Website & Automation Plan

## SEO Recommendation: Service Area Wording

Use this wording on the website:

> Serving Cypress, Los Alamitos, La Palma, Buena Park, and surrounding areas.

Why: it is better than the broader “Cypress, CA and nearby Orange County communities” because it includes the specific city modifiers people are likely to search, while still preserving a local, focused feel.

## Recommended Launch Stack

### Phase 1 — Lowest-friction MVP
- Static website
- Primary CTA: Book a Free Meet & Greet
- Phone/SMS CTA: 714-576-0150
- Lead form opens a pre-filled SMS to Michael Morrison
- Google Business Profile
- Manual payments via Stripe Payment Links, Square, Zelle, or Venmo Business

### Phase 2 — Professional No-Code Operations
- Calendly or TidyCal for meet-and-greet calls
- Google Forms, Tally, or website form connected to Google Sheets
- Zapier or Make automation:
  - New inquiry → Google Sheet row
  - New inquiry → email notification
  - New inquiry → auto-reply to client
  - Completed service → review request SMS/email
- Stripe or Square for digital invoices/payment links

### Phase 3 — Pet Care Platform
When the client roster grows, consider Time To Pet, Scout, or a similar pet-care operations platform for scheduling, client portals, invoices, visit reports, and care notes.
